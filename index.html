Our Goal

In the city of Oxford there are many children with autism and the resources to help these children are scarce. The Oxford Autism Foundation hopes to raise funds to provide the much needed support parents and children of autism need. Our objective is to provide affordable ABA Therapy to every child that needs it. Many children who live in Oxford travel to Tupelo or Southaven to receive this much needed therapy. Others have been on waiting lists and are not receiving any of these services.
